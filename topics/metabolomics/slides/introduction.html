Metabolomics - Newly emerging field of 'omics' research - Comprehensive and simultaneous systematic determination of metabolite levels in the metabolome and their changes over time as a consequence of stimuli - Metabolome - Refers to the complete set of small-molecule metabolites - Dynamic - Metabolites - Intermediates and products of metabolism - Examples include antibiotics, pigments, carbohydrates, fatty acids and amino acids -
