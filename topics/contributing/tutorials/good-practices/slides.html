--- layout: tutorial_slides logo: "GTN" title: "Good practices to run a workshop" questions: - "What do we need to take care when running a workshop?" - "What are the good practices?" objectives: - "Reflect on the things to do before, during and after a workshop" time_estimation: "1h/1d" key_points: - "Prepare your workshop" - "Take care of your trainees" contributors: - bebatut - bgruening - shiltemann --- ### Before a workshop - Venue - Go and check the room - Ask for big screens - Technical infrastructure - Install the needed tools and data - Check the scalability for the number of participants - Test the tutorials on the infrastructure - Have a backup plan! --- ### During a workshop - Beginning - Do an introduction round (with throwing a ball) - Ask for the data needs Everything is about networking! --- ### During a workshop - During - Repeat again and again - Ask questions regularly - Ask "Who is not finished?" and use post-it - Explain and re-explain the file formats by grouping them - Reads the [teaching recommendation](https://carpentries.github.io/instructor-training/22-practices/) of Software Carpentry - Interact with your trainees .image-50[![](../../images/interactivity.jpg)] --- ### End/After a workshop - Feedback questionnaire - Email with all links
